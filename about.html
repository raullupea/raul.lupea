Our Story

Welcome to ManOn

It all came to life with a passion for this beautiful sport that is Football.

My name is Raul Lupea and i am the founder and the owner of ManOn .

As a child i remember playing football all day long ,after school ,at the school ,at home ,everywhere,one day in july 2024 i told myself that i need to do what i love in life ,and because i didn't make it as a professional footballer i still wanted to feel the passion for this sport and decided to build the first 5G AstroTurf in Aylesbury /Buckhinghamshire /UK .
